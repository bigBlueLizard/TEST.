E-Card Kaiji

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • Each player is given five cards. Player 1 is given four Citizen cards and one Emperor card, Player 2 is given four Citizen cards and one Slave card, Card Rules
  • Emperor Card
  • The Emperor card beats a Citizen card, but will lose out to the Slave card.
  • Slave Card
  • The Slave card loses to a Citizen card, but will beat the Emperor card.
  • Citizen Card
  • Ties with other Citizen cards, but will lose to the Emperor.
